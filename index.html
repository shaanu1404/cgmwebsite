Who are we

Cosmos Green Metals is an Entrepreneurial company and leading manufacturer, trader and exporter of Non-Ferrous Metals. We also deals in Ferrous Metals and Mineral Lump. Cosmos Green Metals, is a new kind of scrap metal recycling and waste handling company with a business model that is truly revolutionary in the industry.

Through state-of-the-art facilities, equipment, technology, procedures and the collective knowledge of our team members, CGM meets the scrap disposal needs of all its customers and the ecological needs of the environment.

CGM has a pillars ethics, respect for life, sustainability, social and financial responsibility and the ongoing search for quality and innovation, committed to transform the future.
